About this site

Welcome to this fan-driven gallery dedicated to Job Change Log, a webtoon that captures the complexities of navigating professional life, personal growth, and relationships.

What is "Job Change Log"?

The story follows the lives of individuals at different crossroads in their careers. Whether it's the struggle of finding a first job, the courage to leave a stable position for a passion, or managing the social dynamics of an office, the series resonates with anyone trying to find their place in the modern world. Its blend of relatable drama and distinct art style makes it a standout title for fans of slice-of-life storytelling.
